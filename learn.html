How to Choose an Air Purifier

Start with room size, then compare CADR, filter type, noise level and replacement filter cost. For bedrooms, prioritize quiet sleep mode. For living rooms, prioritize higher CADR and auto mode. For pet homes, combine HEPA filtration with activated carbon.

Air Purifiers for Pet Owners

Pet-focused shoppers care about dander, hair and odor. Product pages should explain filter layers, carbon deodorization, washable pre-filters and practical placement near sofas, litter areas or pet beds.

When to Replace Your Filter

Replacement timing depends on usage, pollution level and filter type. Build a filter-subscription or reminder flow to increase customer satisfaction and repeat purchase value.
